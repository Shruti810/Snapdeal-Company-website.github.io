Introduction of Snapdeal Company

Snapdeal is one of India's leading e-commerce companies
with its headquarters located in New Dehi.Snapdeal was
launched in 2010,a time when the e-commerce market in India
was at a nascent stage.The company was co-founded by Kunal
Bhal and Rohit Bansal. Snapdeal currently offers more than
60 million products across various categories such as mobiles
Women's fashion,sports,fitness and financia services.The company
has more than 3 lakh sellers on its e-commerce platform.

Recruitment Process

    Snapdeal conducts 4-5 rounds to recruit new employees.
  • Written Round
  • Technical Round
    •      >Technical Round 1
         >Technical Round 2
         >Technical Round 3
  • HR Round

  • Written Round:
    •    The Written round consists of two major sections namely General Aptitude Test and Coding Test.
       Different sections of the General Aptitude test are :
         >Mathematical Reasoning part.
         >Logical Reasoning Part.
         >Verbal Ability test.
       In the Coding test sometimes they ask to code puzzles like
       (C and C++ puzzles).
  • Technical Round
    • -   To maximize your chances you should be prepared with Data structures, Algorithms, DBMS, and Operating Systems.
    -   Networking, OOPs concepts and a programming language of your choice.
    -   Students from branches other than CS should prepare for other to subjects related to their branches.
    -   CS students will be expected to write codes in this round.
  • HR Round
    • -   They asked general HR questions like:
    -   1.Tell me about Yourself.
    -   2.Why Snapdeal?
    -   3.What are your strength and weakness?
    -   4.Questions from resume.
